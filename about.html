About CASI

Carnegie Mellon AI Safety Initiative

Our Mission

CASI seeks to spark interest in and facilitate research about AI safety and alignment. We're building a vibrant community of researchers, students, and enthusiasts working together to ensure AI systems remain beneficial as they become more capable.
