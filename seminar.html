CASI AI Safety Seminar

A Seminar on AI safety and security

The CASI AI Safety Seminar is a research-level, technical seminar covering topics in AI safety and security. It is geared towards PhD students, postdocs, professors, and others actively engaged in ML research.

Each session features paper presentations and in-depth discussions on cutting-edge safety and security research.

Food is provided.
