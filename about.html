About CASI

Carnegie Mellon AI Safety Initiative

Our Mission

CASI seeks to spark interest in and facilitate research about AI safety and alignment. We're building a vibrant community of researchers, students, and enthusiasts working together to ensure AI systems remain beneficial as they become more capable.

💬 Make sure to join our discord server for more updates and discussions.

What We Do

  • Facilitate cutting-edge AI safety and alignment research
  • Host workshops, talks, and educational events
  • Connect students with research opportunities
  • Build a community of AI safety researchers and enthusiasts
  • Collaborate with other AI safety organizations
